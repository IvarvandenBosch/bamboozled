The problem

The problem we’re talking about is fast fashion. Fast fashion are clothes that get produced as fast and cheap as possible. The problem is that the process causes a lot of pollution and toxic water that gets dumped into the sea. The amount of CO2 that gets released is enormous. With every KG textile, 10 KG CO2 gets released in the air. Every year 79 billion cubic meters fresh water gets used during the production of clothes. We, Bamboozled try to reduce these horrible numbers by convincing companies to use bamboo. Bamboo uses less water and CO2 during production.

About us

As many of you know, fast fashion is a big problem in the world. Our orginasation, bamboozled, was founded because we were so shocked and wanted to take action. Within the clothing industry, big mistakes are made for example: the spieces of plants that are being used. We want to solve these problems for a better environment.
